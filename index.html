How to!

download the app from the app store or go to passdown.com

create a profile and choose donation preference

find a student’s wish list in a compatible size and start a care package of 5 to 8 items

take photos of your items and passdown will send you a mailing label

mail out your donation and give yourself a pat on the back! don’t forget to write it off your taxes!
